About us

The LittleKo is the principal non-profit, non-government organisation dedicated to the conservation and effective management of the wild koala and its habitat. Our organisation was formed in 2000. Since our founding, and under the direction of LittleKo Chair Anna Kotova, the LittleKo has grown from a small group of people interested in researching koala disease, to a well-known, global organisation with a strong track record in strategic koala research, conservation and community education. The LittleKo is completely self- funded and does not take one dollar from any level of Government. This gives the LittleKo an incredibly free voice for the Koala and for the protection of its habitat.
